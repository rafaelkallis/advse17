Why do we make assumptions?

Cognitive dissonance?

Mental discomfort when holding multiple contradictory views.
Individuals may reduce inconsistency by searching for information which re-confirms their views.

Confirmation bias?

Tendency of testing hypotheses in ways that tend to confirm rather than disconfirm them.

Impact of confirmation bias on software engineering

hypothesis testing ⇔ software testing
lower confirmation bias levels ⇒ higher test effectiveness
higher test effectiveness ⇒ more bugs can be discovered
more bugs discovered + productivity ⇒ more bugs fixed

What causes confirmation bias?

Bad hypothesis testing skills and weak logical reasoning.
Incomplete software specifications.

Empirical Evidence

People with higher education degree exhibited less confirmation bias.
More complete specifications, including expected failure behavior, caused a decrease in confirmation bias.

Conclusions

Strong logical reasoning lead to a higher test effectiveness.
Companies should provide training on logical reasoning and make developers aware of confirmation bias.
